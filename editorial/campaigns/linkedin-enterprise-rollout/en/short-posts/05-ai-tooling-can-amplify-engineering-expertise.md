# Post 05: a good AI tool can amplify engineering expertise

## Copy

There is a common fear:

if engineers rely on AI too much, they will stop thinking.

That can absolutely happen if the tool is just an uncontrolled chat.

But with a well-structured runtime, I keep seeing a different outcome:

the tool starts amplifying engineering expertise.

Why:

- decisions leave a trace
- verify and review artifacts stay visible
- facts are easier to separate from guesses
- a study mode turns exploration into a structured activity
- knowledge becomes less trapped inside individual heads

This is one of the most interesting effects for me.

A strong AI runtime can be both a delivery tool and a knowledge amplifier.
