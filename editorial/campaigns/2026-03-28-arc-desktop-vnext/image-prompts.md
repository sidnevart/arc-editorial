## habr-chat-first-workspace

Create a premium editorial illustration for a technical article about an AI workspace product. The concept is “conversation turns into structured output.” Show a calm, modern desktop interface where a central chat thread transforms into cards labeled plan, document, diagram, demo, and simulation. Avoid generic neon cyberpunk. Use clear product-design aesthetics, white and graphite surfaces, restrained blue and orange accents, soft depth, premium modern UI composition, high readability, and a trustworthy feel. No logos, no text-heavy interface, no fantasy elements.

## habr-agent-presets

Create an editorial illustration for an article about three built-in AI agents with different responsibility levels. Show three distinct but coherent agent cards in one premium interface: Study, Work, Hero. Study should feel educational and guided, Work collaborative and structured, Hero autonomous and powerful. Keep the style modern, calm, product-focused, and suitable for a serious engineering article. Avoid clichés like robots or humanoid AI. Use abstract UI motifs, clean hierarchy, and subtle color differentiation.
