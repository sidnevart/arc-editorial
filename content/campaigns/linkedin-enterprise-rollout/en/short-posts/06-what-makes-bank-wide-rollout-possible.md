# Post 06: what makes a bank-wide rollout possible

## Copy

The real enterprise question about AI tooling is not:

“Can we launch it?”

It is:

“Can we scale it without losing control?”

That is where many AI dev tools break.

They may work as personal assistants, but they are not ready as organisational systems.

To scale beyond a small group, you need:

- explicit modes
- a predictable task lifecycle
- memory and rules
- approvals and risk gates
- docs and operator experience
- observable runtime behaviour

Without those layers, you get local magic.
With them, you get a chance at systemic adoption.
